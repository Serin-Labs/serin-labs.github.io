Mitsubishi HVAC Controller

This package aims to make it simpler to make your Mitsubishi HVAC unit smarter, more private, and easier to control. This package brings local, cloud-free control to your HVAC system using community driven open source software.

Why?

  • 100% Local Control – Your data stays in your home. No cloud, no fees, no privacy worries.
  • Open Source – Powered by ESPHome and the community. Inspect, modify, and contribute.
  • Compact & Clean – Installs inside your mini-split for a seamless look.
  • DIY or Pre-Assembled – Build it yourself or order a ready-to-go kit from the shop.

Find full setup guides and a YAML config generator so you can customize your device for features like remote temperature sensors, vane control, and advanced diagnostics.

Quick Start

  1. Pick Your Hardware: Choose from M5Stack Atom S3 Lite, NanoC6, or order a kit with cable and pre-flashed firmware.
  2. Optional: Flash the Firmware: Use the YAML generator and ESPHome Dashboard, or flash a pre-built firmware with ESPHome Web. This step is optional if the kit was purchased from the shop.
  3. Connect to Wi-Fi: Plug in via USB-C and use the Wi-Fi setup tool, or join the device’s Wi-Fi and visit 192.168.4.1. Enter your Wi-Fi credentials.
  4. Install in Your HVAC: Power off, connect the CN105-to-Grove cable, tuck the device inside, and restore power.
  5. Web UI: Access the device’s local IP for direct control. If you don't desire further integration, you can stop here!
  6. Integrate with Home Assistant: The device will appear in ESPHome and be auto-discovered by Home Assistant.
For more detailed instructions, visit the setup guide.
