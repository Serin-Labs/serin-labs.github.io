Support

The fastest route to an answer depends on the question. Start with the card that matches yours.

From blank board to working thermostat:

  • Flash the firmware — free, in your browser
  • ESPHome setup (Home Assistant)

  • Wiring reference — connecting to the CN105 port

Something Not Working

Start with the guides — they cover the common failure points:

  • Troubleshooting — flashing, Wi-Fi, CN105, pairing
  • Check Your Model — type your model number for an instant compatibility answer.
  • Wiring — pinouts and cable checks

Bought a controller or cable? Shipping, returns, refunds, and replacements all go through Etsy and are covered by its purchase protection — the full terms are on our store policies page.

  • Message the shop on Etsy — fastest route for order issues

Technical Questions & Community

Anything the guides don't cover gets answered on GitHub, in the open:

  • GitHub Discussions — ask a question (free account)
  • GitHub Issues — report a bug
  • Contact form — private questions, no account needed

Firmware Updates

The firmware is open source and free, and so are updates — there's no subscription attached to the hardware. After the first USB flash, updates arrive over Wi-Fi: ESPHome pushes them from your dashboard, and the HomeKit-compatible firmware updates from its web UI. You can also reflash over USB anytime, including to switch platforms.

Before You Ask

A few details make any question much faster to answer:

  • Your indoor unit model (on the sticker under the front panel — e.g. MSZ-GL06NA).
  • Which firmware you flashed: ESPHome or HomeKit-compatible.
  • Where it fails: flashing, Wi-Fi, pairing, or control of the unit.
  • Anything the troubleshooting page already ruled out.
